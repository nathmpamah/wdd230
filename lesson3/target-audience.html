Target Audience Description

The purpose of this page is to highlight the type of users that we anticipate to visit our site in order to obtain the weather information necessary for them to plan their daily activities. This can range from young adults checking the weather to determine what activities would be suitable for them to participate in or people with medical conditions that are affected by the ups and downs that come with the changes in weather conditions.

Personas

Femi Otedola


  • Occupation: Businessman
  • Demographics & Education: 57 year old male, $150-200k, married, Masters Degree.
  • Reason for using the site: Loves hiking and having business meetings in outdoor open spaces. He needs weather info to determine dressing.
  • Social: Business events, Cocktail parties, camping/hiking.
  • Technology: Macbook Pro, iphone, and iPad
  • Quote: "It's a good day to be good."

Maggie Smith


  • Occupation: Farmer

  • Quote: "Make hay while the sun shines."
