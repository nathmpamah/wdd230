Site Name

The site name will be Nate's Weather.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Nate's Weather is to provide accurate weather forcasting services to users. This could include information for cloudy/rainy weather conditions, snow, high/low temperatures for the hour and day. The users can come to the site and be confident that they can obtain the information necessary for them to know the right clothing to put on, the kind of activities to plan, and for some, which type of vehicle they should employ. The services are expected to run for 24 hours a day and 7 days a week.
